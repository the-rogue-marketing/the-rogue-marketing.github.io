## 🎯 Which Provider Should You Choose?

### Choose **Google Gemini** if you want:
- ✅ The **cheapest budget model** (Flash-Lite at $0.10/M)
- ✅ **Generous free tier** for prototyping
- ✅ Best **multimodal** capabilities (text, audio, image, video)
- ✅ **Context caching** that saves up to 90%

### Choose **OpenAI** if you want:
- ✅ The **largest ecosystem** (ChatGPT, plugins, tool integrations)
- ✅ Strong **GPT-4.1** at competitive pricing with 1M context
- ✅ **Dedicated reasoning** with o3 series
- ✅ Best **image generation** API

### Choose **xAI Grok** if you want:
- ✅ The **largest context window** (2M tokens)
- ✅ **Cheapest reasoning** model (Grok 4.1 Fast)
- ✅ **Free credits** ($175/month)
- ✅ Built-in **live search** from X/Twitter

### Choose **Anthropic Claude** if you want:
- ✅ Best **safety and alignment**
- ✅ **Strongest instruction-following**
- ✅ Excellent at **long, nuanced writing**
- ✅ Enterprise-grade via **AWS Bedrock / GCP Vertex**
